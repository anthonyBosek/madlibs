MadLibs!
MadLibs is a fun and interactive command-line game where players can create silly stories by filling in the blanks in our story templates. Let your creativity run wild and enjoy the laughter-inducing narratives generated by your input!

Features
Choose from a variety of themed templates, such as fantasy, adventure, mystery, and more.
Fill in the blanks with different parts of speech, such as nouns, verbs, adjectives, ect.
Experience endless story combinations for endless entertainment.
Easy-to-use command-line interface for a seamless gaming experience.

Commands:
-help: help menu

-exit: exit the program

-start: start the program

-back: return to options menu

-new: create a new story

Author can delete account in options menu.
